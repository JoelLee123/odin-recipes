Ingredients

  • Lean ground mince
  • Onion and garlic (2 cloves)
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce (2 cans) and tomato paste (2 cans)
  • Sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Parsely, basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta
  • Egg

Method

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
